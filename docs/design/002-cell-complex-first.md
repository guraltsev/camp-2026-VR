# 002 - Cell Complex First

## What did we decide?

Model the runtime around cell complexes from the beginning, with vertical prism
cells as the first implemented cell type.

## Why now?

Prism cells give students a true 3D room-scale experience while keeping the
first compiler, collision, portals, and tests approachable.

## What are we explicitly not deciding?

We are not implementing arbitrary 3D polyhedral cell complexes, curvature
calculations, global geodesic solvers, or QR-based authoring yet.

## What would cause us to revisit this?

Revisit after prism movement, portal crossing, ray tools, and classroom
hardening are working reliably.
