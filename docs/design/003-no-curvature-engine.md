# 003 - No Curvature Engine

## What did we decide?

The first runtime is an exploration environment, not a theorem engine. It will
not compute curvature, Gauss-Bonnet values, holonomy, Euler characteristic, or
point-to-point geodesics.

## Why now?

The course interpretation belongs to the instructor and students. The software
should provide reliable movement, portals, rays, markers, traces, and
measurements without turning those experiences into automatic theorem
explanations.

## What are we explicitly not deciding?

We are not ruling out future mathematical analysis tools. We are only keeping
them out of the first runtime.

## What would cause us to revisit this?

Revisit only after the explorable environment is stable and a later stage asks
for analysis tools explicitly.
